General rules for posting

What to Submit

Anything that is interesting, belongs to given feed and you think that others might find it interesting as well.

  • Make titles descriptive and on point, if possible use the original title.
  • Provide short description if possible, if possible use the original description.
  • Provide original source not reports or mentions on other sites.
  • Don't submit too many links and don't submit same link more times.
  • Follow rules for posting of individual feeds.

General rules for comments

  • Don't say things you wouldn't say personally.
  • Show respect to others.
  • Avoid vulgarity.
  • Assume good faith.
  • Don't complain about the link or other comments. Simple vote down should do the thing and avoids pointless discussions.
